What we do

Syndicate How it works The results

Content Syndication is an automated MarTech solution for premium digital content distribution.

While brands commit enormous resources to crafting their product message, their e-commerce partners cannot keep up with the variety of content formats, thus using the lowest common denominator for product pages: text and basic galleries.

Cognitive research has shown that the human brain processes images quicker than words, suggesting that the visualisation of product features is particularly critical for a faster understanding.

Stunning imagery, engaging video, immersive 3D using Augmented Reality, and even vital specifications – all empower shoppers to make informed purchases.

Content syndication delivers an easy-to-implement automation for brands, amplifying their content for retailers, who in turn receive double digit conversion upticks by enabling best-in-class product experiences for their shoppers.

By plugging in to Flixmedia, brands deliver stunning product content to thousands of retailers in our network.

Retailers therefore receive thousands of engaging product experiences (INpages, MiniSites, Hotspots) to their websites, using a single line of code from Flixmedia.
